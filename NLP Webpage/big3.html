Building a Classifier on Saad, Rogges, and Javed's Messages
The Eye Test
When testing a model, testing it against obvious cases can help determine whether it has any merit as a model or not. This is because if a model misses correctly classifying the simplest of test cases, what hope is there of trusting the classification of more complex cases.
Phrases correctly attributed to Rogges: Fuck small retailers, Amazon is the greatest; Why not fountain pens; Messi is better than Ronaldo; You got Timmys money.
Phrases correctly attributed to Saad: Christianity was supposed to be Islam; Joanna Nash made another lab tech cry.
Phrases correctly attributed to Javed: TD won't raise my credit limit; I wish I could stay in Waterloo engineering forever.
